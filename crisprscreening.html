Visualising the data

The boxplot demonstrates the 3 genes with a significant differences in cell count fold change were detected in the presence or absence of a cancer diver mutation (p<0.05)

  • When gene G is knocked-out there was a significant increase in cell count fold change in the presence of the cancer driver mutation 6
    • This suggests cell viability is maintained indicating cancer driver mutation 6 compensates for the function of gene G
  • When gene E is knocked-out there was a significant decrease in cell count fold change in the presence of the cancer driver mutation 8
    • This suggests cell viability is reduced indicating cancer driver mutation 8 relies on gene E for cell viability
  • When gene A is knocked-out there was a significant increase in cell count fold change in the presence of the cancer driver mutation 9
    • This suggests cell viability is maintained indicating cancer driver mutation 9 ompensates for the function of gene A
  • significance not retained after fdr adjustment
